## One-Page Project Summary

**Project:** Melioidosis Exposure Awareness & Reporting Simulator

**Purpose:**  
Educational simulation demonstrating how healthcare systems recognize and
respond to potential rare-pathogen exposure events through communication and
reporting pathways.

**Key Skills Demonstrated**
- Systems thinking
- Occupational safety awareness
- Epidemiology-to-clinical translation
- Ethical data handling
- Clear technical communication

**Relevance to Medicine**
This project aligns with Internal Medicine by emphasizing patient safety,
interdisciplinary coordination, and prevention of avoidable harm through
systems-level awareness.
